Our History

The Eschen Chess Club was founded by a small group of passionate chess enthusiasts in the heart of Liechtenstein. What started as casual games in a local cafe has grown into a vibrant community of players from all skill levels — from curious beginners learning their first openings to experienced competitors pursuing master-level ratings.

Located in the scenic village of Eschen, our club embodies the spirit of intellectual competition and camaraderie that makes chess the greatest game ever invented.

Our Mission

To promote the game of chess in Eschen and the wider Liechtenstein community. We aim to provide a welcoming space where players of every age and ability can learn, improve, and compete. Through regular club nights, tournaments, and community events, we strive to make chess accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Understanding ELO Ratings

ELO ratings are a numerical system used to measure the relative skill levels of players in competitive games. Every player starts with a rating of 1200 and gains or loses points based on match results.
